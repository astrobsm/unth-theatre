**SUBJECT: MANDATORY WEEKLY UPDATE OF DUTY ROSTERS ON THE THEATRE MANAGEMENT APPLICATION**

---

1. **Introduction**

   Hospital Management, in its continuing effort to strengthen the coordination of surgical and perioperative activities across the Theatre Complex, has reviewed the prevailing practice of duty roster submission and has taken a firm decision regarding its standardisation. This memorandum communicates that decision and the obligations arising therefrom.

2. **Management Decision**

   It has been decided that, with immediate effect, **the duty roster for every relevant unit must be created and updated weekly directly on the Theatre Management Application.** This applies to, but is not limited to, the Department of Anaesthesia, the Theatre Nursing Department, the Department of Pharmacy, the Anaesthetic Technicians, the Porters, the Cleaners, and the Recovery Room Nursing team.

3. **Weekly Deadline**

   The duty roster for the forthcoming week **must be fully entered and uploaded on the Application not later than 4:00 p.m. (16:00 hours) every Saturday.** This deadline allows Management and all dependent units adequate time to plan, allocate, and coordinate activities ahead of the new working week.

4. **Rationale**

   Seamless coordination of theatre activities depends substantially on the timely availability of accurate duty information. Late or absent rosters disrupt case scheduling, staff allocation, anaesthetic cover, pharmacy support, and patient flow, and ultimately place patient safety and institutional efficiency at avoidable risk. Compliance with this directive is therefore not optional; it is integral to the smooth running of the Theatre Complex.

5. **Consequence of Non-Compliance**

   Management wishes to state, with all due respect to colleagues and Heads of Department, that:

   a. **Failure to create and upload the weekly duty roster on the Application by the stipulated deadline of 4:00 p.m. on Saturday shall be regarded as an anti-progress action and a queriable offence.**

   b. Any unit, Head of Department, or responsible officer who fails to comply shall **automatically be issued a Management Query** through the Application, requiring a written explanation within the period stipulated therein.

   c. The Application will continue to monitor compliance automatically, and records of late or non-submission will be retained for review by Theatre Management and the Medical Advisory Committee.

6. **Appeal for Cooperation**

   Management deeply values the professionalism and dedication of all Heads of Department and their teams. This directive is issued not in a spirit of sanction, but in our collective interest of building a coordinated, efficient, and patient-centred theatre service. Your prompt and sustained cooperation is earnestly solicited.

7. **Effective Date**

   This directive takes effect from the date of this memorandum. The first roster governed by the Saturday 4:00 p.m. deadline shall be the roster for the week commencing the Monday immediately following receipt of this memorandum.

Please be guided accordingly.

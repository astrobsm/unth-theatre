RE: STAFF ONBOARDING ON THE OPERATIVE RESOURCE MANAGER (ORM) PLATFORM
    AND COMMENCEMENT OF DIGITAL DUTY ROSTERS WITH EFFECT FROM
    SUNDAY, 10TH MAY 2026

Dear Sir/Madam,

Sequel to the deployment of the Operative Resource Manager (ORM) — the unified digital
platform now serving the Main Theatre Complex of UNTH Ituku-Ozalla — I write to formally
solicit your support in driving full staff adoption across your department.

The platform consolidates patient scheduling, pre-operative review, theatre allocation,
intra-operative documentation (WHO Surgical Safety Checklist), PACU handover, blood and
drug requests, equipment checkout, fault and emergency alerts, the live theatre radio
service, departmental announcements and the weekly duty roster — all in one place,
accessible from any phone, tablet or computer, online or offline.

To this end, I respectfully request the following:

1.  STAFF PROFILE CREATION
    Kindly direct every member of staff in your department — clinical and non-clinical —
    to register an individual profile on the platform without delay. Each staff member
    will use the role and department that matches their substantive posting; the system
    will automatically generate their unique staff code on approval.

2.  COMMENCEMENT OF DIGITAL DUTY ROSTERS — SUNDAY, 10TH MAY 2026
    With effect from Sunday, 10th May 2026, all departmental duty rosters (call,
    morning, afternoon and night shifts; on-call consultants; standby teams) shall be
    created and maintained on the ORM platform. Manual / paper rosters will run only
    in parallel during the transition fortnight and will be retired thereafter. Heads
    of Department, Unit Heads and Roster Officers are kindly requested to ensure that
    the roster for the week beginning Sunday, 10th May 2026 is published on the
    platform on or before Saturday, 9th May 2026, 12:00 noon, and that subsequent
    weekly rosters are uploaded every Thursday before 12:00 noon as already
    standardised.

3.  PHYSICAL ONBOARDING MEETINGS — FROM NEXT WEEK
    A dedicated physical onboarding session will be held for each department from
    next week. The schedule will be circulated separately by this office in
    consultation with each Head of Department. Attendance is mandatory for all
    departmental staff; laptops, tablets or smartphones should be brought along so
    that profiles, rosters and module access can be configured live during the
    session.

4.  PLATFORM ACCESS LINK
    The platform is available at:

        https://unth-theatre-mai.vercel.app

    It is also installable as a Progressive Web App on Android, iOS and Windows
    devices (use the "Install App" prompt or the browser's "Add to Home Screen"
    option). The app continues to function in offline mode and will synchronise
    once connectivity is restored.

5.  ROLE NOMENCLATURE — ALIGNMENT WITH HOSPITAL PRACTICE
    Please note the following role-label alignment now reflected on the platform,
    which Heads of Nursing and Anaesthesia are kindly requested to bring to the
    attention of their staff during onboarding:
        •  "Scrub Nurse"          →  Peri-Operative Nurse
        •  "Recovery Room Nurse"  →  Nurse Anaesthetist

    Existing staff records are unaffected; only the displayed role title has been
    updated.

6.  SUPPORT
    For technical assistance during registration or roster upload, departmental
    super-users and the ICT helpdesk will be on hand throughout the onboarding
    fortnight. Issues may also be reported in-app via Settings → Fault Alerts.

Your usual co-operation in ensuring 100% staff onboarding and timely roster publication
will go a long way in enhancing patient safety, transparency and operational efficiency
in our theatre complex.

Thank you.

Why this app exists

The Operative Resource Manager (ORM) replaces paper books, WhatsApp chats and verbal handovers with one auditable, real-time system that follows every patient from booking to discharge.
⏱ Faster patient flow — fewer cancellations
🔒 Safer — WHO checklist, swab counts, allergy alerts
📊 Transparent — every action logged with name + timestamp
⚖ Accountable — disciplinary queries are evidence-based

How everyone logs in

  1. Open https://unth-theatre-mai.vercel.app on any phone, tablet or PC
  2. Click Sign Up → enter your full name, staff code and role
  3. Wait for Admin approval (you will receive an alert)
  4. Log in with your username + password — change password on first login
  5. The app installs as a PWA — tap Add to Home Screen for one-tap access
📶 Works offline. Anything you submit while offline is queued and syncs the moment you reconnect — never re-enter data.

Universal rules — apply to everyone

  • 🆔 Never share your login. Every action is signed by your account.
  • ⏰ Start & End Duty on time. Late starts and missed end-of-duty are tracked.
  • 📝 Document at the point of care, not at end-of-shift. Memory is not evidence.
  • 🚨 Acknowledge alerts within 2 minutes of receipt.
  • 🧹 Leave the system tidy: complete checklists, clear holding-area entries, return equipment.
❌ Disciplinary triggers: falsified entries • shared passwords • ignored alerts • incomplete WHO checklists • un-returned equipment.

Disciplinary process — fair & evidence-based

1. Auto-flag
System detects breach (e.g. missed checklist, ignored alert).
2. Query issued
Disciplinary Query appears on your dashboard with full audit trail.
3. Respond
You have 48 hours to respond inside the app. Attach evidence.
4. Review
HOD / Admin reviews response. Outcome is logged. No verbal disputes.

Every query, response and outcome is permanent and exportable.

🔪 Surgeons

Consultants • Senior Registrars • Registrars • House Officers

12 slides — press ↓

Your daily ORM workflow

  1. Start Duty → confirms you are on-site & on-call
  2. Review Booked Surgeries for the day
  3. Complete Pre-op Review for each of your patients
  4. Sign in to WHO Sign-In Checklist before incision
  5. Document Time Out & Sign Out after surgery
  6. Update Operation Notes immediately post-op
  7. End Duty at handover

Booking a surgery — what only YOU can do

  • Surgeries → New Surgery
  • Confirm: patient ID, diagnosis, planned procedure, urgency, special needs (Diathermy / Stirrups / Montrell mattress / Blood)
  • Add full Surgical Team: Consultants → Senior Registrars → Registrars → HOs
  • Specify required blood units if anticipating bleeding > 500 ml
⚠️ Booking without blood request when transfusion is likely is a query offence.

Pre-op review responsibilities

✓ Confirm consent signed & witnessed
✓ Mark surgical site (where applicable)
✓ Review labs & imaging
✓ Document fitness for surgery
✓ Verify NPO status
✓ Patient identification band present

Use the 🎤 Dictate button — voice-to-text is enabled on every form.

WHO Surgical Safety Checklist — non-negotiable

  • Sign-In (before anaesthesia) — patient ID, site, consent, anaesthetic safety check
  • Time-Out (before incision) — team introductions, antibiotic prophylaxis confirmed, critical events anticipated
  • Sign-Out (before patient leaves theatre) — procedure recorded, swab/instrument count confirmed, specimens labelled
❌ No checklist = no incision. Skipping or back-dating any phase is grounds for immediate query.

Cancellations

  • Cancel only via Cancellations module — never verbally
  • State category: Patient-related • Surgical • Anaesthetic • Logistic • Equipment
  • Provide specific reason, not "see notes"
  • If cancellation is preventable, system flags it for monthly audit
💡 Cancellation patterns are reviewed monthly by the Theatre Manager.

Mortality registry

  1. Any intra-op or 24h post-op death must be entered within 4 hours
  2. Initial entry: patient ID, time, immediate cause
  3. Within 72 hours: complete the audit form (M&M)
  4. Findings auto-pulled into monthly mortality meeting
Failure to log within 4 hours triggers automatic disciplinary query to the consultant in charge.

Operation notes & post-op orders

  • Operation note within 30 minutes of skin closure
  • Include: indication, findings, procedure, blood loss, specimens, complications, post-op plan
  • Prescriptions go through Rx Approvals — pharmacy will not dispense without your e-signature
  • Hand over to PACU nurse in person + in app

Emergency surgeries

  1. Use 🚨 Emergency Booking from the sidebar
  2. System auto-pages on-call team via Team Availability module
  3. Pre-anaesthetic review can be condensed but never skipped
  4. Lab workup tracked via Emergency Lab Workup
✅ Emergency response time is logged and benchmarked — it protects you, not punishes you.

Benefits to YOU as a surgeon

🧠 No more lost paper notes — search any case in seconds
📈 Auto-generated logbook for FMCS / FWACS revalidation
📊 Personal cancellation, complication & turnaround stats
⚖ Evidence-based defence against unfair complaints

💉 Anaesthetists

Consultants • Senior Registrars • Registrars • Technicians

12 slides — press ↓

Your daily ORM workflow

  1. Start Duty → check duty roster & theatre allocation
  2. Review Pre-op Reviews assigned to you
  3. Complete Anaesthesia Setup for your theatre
  4. Run Intra-op Charting (vitals, drugs, fluids, events)
  5. Hand over to PACU with vitals & instructions
  6. End Duty at handover

Pre-anaesthetic assessment

  • Open patient → Pre-op Review → "Anaesthetic Review" tab
  • Document: airway grade, ASA, allergies, comorbidities, prior anaesthetic history
  • Confirm fasting, consent, premedication
  • For under-15s, the Braden Scale is now optional — use Braden Q if clinically indicated
No surgery can proceed until your e-signature appears on the pre-op review.

Anaesthesia Setup module

  • Confirm machine check (suction, O₂, agents, circuits)
  • Drug tray prepared & labelled
  • Emergency drugs available (atropine, ephedrine, adrenaline, suxamethonium)
  • Difficult airway cart accessible if needed
  • Oxygen status — check the Power Status / O₂ Readiness board before any case

Intra-operative charting

📊 Vitals every 5 min auto-prompted
💊 Each drug logged with dose, time, route
💧 Fluids in / blood / output recorded live
⚠️ Critical events logged with timestamp

Use voice dictation when hands are sterile — works on any field.

Medication tracking

  1. Med Tracking module shows every controlled drug you collect
  2. Log usage immediately after administration
  3. Log returns at end of case
  4. Reconcile at end of duty — discrepancies trigger query
Unaccounted controlled drugs = automatic disciplinary action + pharmacy audit.

PACU handover

  • Open PACU module → New Recovery Entry
  • Provide: anaesthetic technique, drugs given, fluid balance, blood loss
  • Specify discharge criteria (Aldrete score)
  • Stay until handover signed by recovery nurse
PACU vitals can trigger alerts — you'll be paged if your patient deteriorates.

Equipment checkout

  • Borrow video laryngoscope, BIS, US machine via Equipment Checkout
  • Return same day — over-due items flag your name on the dashboard
  • Report faults via Fault Alerts → Biomedical team auto-notified

Emergency response

  1. For paged emergencies, accept on Emergency Alerts
  2. Response time is logged from page → acceptance → arrival
  3. Pre-anaesthetic review can be in Emergency Pre-Anaesthetic shortened form
  4. Blood requests: Blood Bank module — mark URGENT

Blood transfusion safety

  • All requests via Blood Bank — verbal requests not honoured
  • Acknowledge receipt with two-person check (anaesthetist + nurse)
  • Adverse reactions: log via Incidents within 1 hour

Benefits to YOU as anaesthetist

⏱ Auto-built anaesthetic chart — no more handwriting
📈 Personal case-mix logbook for SAQ / FRCA
🛡 Evidence trail protects you in adverse events
📋 PACU handover documented & timed

👩‍⚕️ Theatre Nurses

Scrub • Circulating • Recovery

12 slides — press ↓

Your daily ORM workflow

  1. Start Duty → confirms you on-shift
  2. Check Theatre Allocation & Roster
  3. Theatre Setup & Theatre Readiness confirmed
  4. Receive patient — Holding Area check-in
  5. Run WHO checklist with team
  6. Swab / instrument Count in & out
  7. Nurse Handover at end of shift

Theatre Setup & Readiness

  • Setup module lists all instruments / consumables for the case
  • Tick off as you lay out — missing items raise Setup Request automatically
  • Mark theatre as Ready only when sterilisation, suction, light & monitor confirmed
Marking ready when not ready = patient brought into unsafe theatre = major query.

Holding Area

  1. Patient arrives → scan/enter ID into Holding Area
  2. Verify: identity, consent, site mark, NPO, allergies, premed
  3. Note any concerns → escalate via Holding Area Alert
  4. Move to theatre only after surgeon + anaesthetist confirmation

Swab & instrument counts

  • Use Surgeries → Count module
  • Three counts: initial, before closure, final
  • Both scrub and circulator e-sign
  • Discrepancy locks the closure phase until resolved
A retained item where count was marked correct = automatic incident + investigation.

Consumables & BOM

  • Log every implant, suture, blade, etc. into Consumables
  • Bill of Materials auto-generated from your entries → goes to billing
  • Inventory auto-decremented — keeps the store accurate

Under-recording consumables understates the case → patient is under-billed → hospital revenue lost.

Equipment Checkout

  • Any equipment leaving theatre must be checked out in app
  • Returns checked back in same day
  • Faults reported via Fault Alerts immediately

PACU (Recovery)

  1. Receive handover from anaesthetist in app + verbal
  2. Vitals every 15 min — auto-prompts
  3. Score with Aldrete — discharge only when ≥ 9
  4. Trigger alerts if vitals deteriorate

Nurse Handover at shift change

  • Open Nurse Handover → for each active patient
  • Capture: condition, drains, drugs due, alerts, family communications
  • Receiving nurse e-signs to accept
  • System tracks any patient handed over with no signature

Cleaning & turnover

  1. End of case → Cleaning → Start
  2. Cleaner / nurse confirms surfaces, floor, lights wiped
  3. Cleaning → End releases theatre to next case

Turnover times are tracked — fastest theatres get recognition.

Benefits to YOU as nurse

📋 No more endless paper checklists
🛡 Documented count protects you legally
⏰ Auto-handover so nothing is forgotten
📈 Personal case-load report for promotion review

What gets you queried

ActionSeverity
Falsified countMajor
Marked theatre ready when not readyMajor
Patient handed over without e-signModerate
Consumables not loggedModerate
Equipment returned lateMinor

🚪 Porters

Patient transport & theatre logistics

10 slides — press ↓

Your daily ORM workflow

  1. Start Duty at the start of your shift
  2. Check Roster for your theatre allocation
  3. Watch the Call for Patient board
  4. Log every transport with Transport → Start / End
  5. End Duty at handover

If you do not Start Duty, the system thinks you are absent and may auto-page a relief porter.

How patient transport works

  1. Anaesthetist or scrub nurse triggers "Call for Patient"
  2. You see the alert on your phone within 5 seconds
  3. Tap Accept — this stops auto-paging others
  4. Go to ward, identify patient, verify ID band
  5. Tap Transport → Start when leaving the ward
  6. Tap Transport → End on arrival in holding area

Patient identity check — your responsibility

⚠️ Wrong patient brought to theatre = catastrophic event.
  • Verify: Name + Hospital number on ID band
  • Cross-check with the call entry on your phone
  • Confirm operation site mark is present where applicable
  • Ask the ward nurse to e-sign the release in the app

Theatre meals & supplies

  • Use Theatre Meals module to log food deliveries to the lounge
  • Stock movements (linen, gases, scrubs) — log via Sub-Stores → Transfers
  • Never move equipment without an entry — security audits this

Patient transfers between theatres / wards

  1. Use Patient Transfers module
  2. Source location → destination → reason
  3. E-sign on departure & arrival

Reporting faults & hazards

  • Broken trolley, faulty lift, spills → Fault Alerts
  • Power failure on your route → Power Status note
  • Plumbing leak → Plumbing & Water module
Reporting hazards protects patients and you — never punished for raising them.

Emergency response

  1. Emergency calls take priority over routine transport
  2. You'll get a 🚨 alert with patient location
  3. Accept and respond within 3 minutes

Benefits to YOU as porter

📱 No more chasing nurses for instructions
📊 Your transport record proves you were busy
⏰ End-of-month report — recognition for top performers
🛡 Identity verification protects you from blame

🧹 Cleaners & Housekeeping

Theatre turnover • Infection control

10 slides — press ↓

Your daily ORM workflow

  1. Start Duty at the start of your shift
  2. Watch Cleaning → Start alerts (one per finished case)
  3. Complete cleaning → Cleaning → End
  4. Report any biohazards / spillages immediately
  5. End Duty at handover

The cleaning cycle

  1. Surgeon signs out → app sends a "Cleaning Required" alert to your phone
  2. Tap Accept → enter theatre with PPE
  3. Follow the theatre turnover checklist shown in the app:
    • Sharps cleared
    • Linen bagged
    • Surfaces wiped (operating table, lights, machines)
    • Floor mopped
    • Bin liners changed
  4. Tap Cleaning → End — theatre auto-marked available

Why timing matters

Faster, thorough turnover means more patients treated each day.
  • Average target: 20 min for a clean case, 35 min for a contaminated case
  • Times are visible to your supervisor — consistently fast cleaners get recognition
  • Skipped checklist items reset the timer — quality over speed always wins

Biohazards & spillages

  • Log via Incidents module — describe exposure (blood, body fluid)
  • Use the spill kit immediately
  • If you sustain a needle-stick or splash → Incidents → Self-injury within 1 hour
Hiding an exposure puts you at health risk — reporting is protected, not punished.

Linen & laundry

  • Log dirty linen pickups via Laundry module
  • Confirm clean linen receipt
  • Discrepancies are queried by stores

Reporting faults

  • Broken mop bucket, leaking tap, faulty bin → Fault Alerts
  • Empty soap / sanitiser dispensers → same module
  • Plumbing → Plumbing & Water

Infection control checklist

PPE worn
Hand hygiene before & after
Colour-coded mops respected
Disinfectant correctly diluted

Benefits to YOU as cleaner

  • 📈 Your cleaning record demonstrates your contribution
  • 🏆 Top cleaners are publicly recognised each month
  • 🛡 Documented turnover protects you if a case becomes infected
  • 📱 No more arguments over "did you clean theatre 3?" — the app proves it

🧪 CSSD • Biomedical • Stores

Sterilisation • Equipment • Inventory

10 slides — press ↓

CSSD workflow

  1. Start Duty
  2. Receive used instruments via CSSD Inventory → Return
  3. Process via CSSD Sterilization — log cycle, autoclave, indicator results
  4. Mark sets as Ready in CSSD Readiness
  5. Issue to theatre via CSSD Inventory → Issue

Sterilisation log integrity

  • Every cycle: load contents, temperature, time, indicator outcome
  • Failed indicator → set quarantined → cannot be issued
  • Audit logs are retained for 5 years — required for ISO compliance
Issuing an unsterile set is a sentinel event. The cycle log is your defence.

Biomedical engineers

  • Watch Fault Alerts for equipment in your scope
  • Acknowledge → log diagnosis → log resolution
  • Schedule preventive maintenance via inventory item maintenance interval
  • Decommission obsolete equipment via Inventory → Edit (status: RETIRED)

Power, water, oxygen monitoring

  • Power Status — log generator runs, fuel consumption
  • Power Maintenance — schedule and log servicing
  • Power Readiness — daily readiness check
  • Plumbing & Water — log faults and supply
  • Oxygen Control — alerts auto-fire when O₂ levels dip

Inventory & sub-stores

  1. Inventory module — central store master list
  2. Sub-Stores — per-theatre / per-department stock
  3. Bulk uploads via Excel — see Bulk Upload Guide
  4. Reorder levels trigger automatic alerts to procurement
  5. Expiry dates flag yellow at 90 days, red at 30 days

Stock transfers

  • Stock Transfers for moving items between sub-stores
  • Both sender and receiver e-sign
  • Discrepancies logged for monthly reconciliation

Equipment checkout audit

  • Daily review of Equipment Checkout
  • Items overdue >24h → page user
  • Items overdue >7 days → escalate to HOD

Benefits to YOU as CSSD/Biomedical/Stores

📊 Live inventory — no more end-of-month surprises
🛡 Sterilisation logs protect you legally
🔧 Maintenance schedule auto-generated

🛡 Administrators

Theatre Manager • HODs • System Admins

10 slides — press ↓

Your overview tools

  • Dashboard — live theatre status, today's surgeries, alerts
  • Reports & Analytics — daily / weekly / monthly summaries
  • Staff Effectiveness — individual & team performance
  • Mortality Registry — M&M data ready for committee
  • Disciplinary Queries — open & resolved cases

User management

  1. User Management → review pending sign-ups
  2. Approve, set role, set staff code
  3. Reject with reason if unauthorised
  4. Reset password on request via Auth → Reset Password
Approving a user with the wrong role is a security incident — verify identity first.

Roster management

  • Upload weekly roster via Excel — separate templates for nurses, anaesthetists, porters, cleaners, technicians
  • Auto-allocates staff to theatres + shifts
  • Last-minute swaps logged with reason
  • Absence patterns flagged automatically

Handling disciplinary queries

  1. Open Disciplinary Queries module
  2. Review the auto-evidence (timestamps, before/after states)
  3. Read staff response (within 48h)
  4. Decide: Dismissed • Verbal Warning • Written Warning • HOD Referral
  5. Outcome saved permanently — visible to staff
Decisions are auditable — protects both staff and management from bias claims.

Anonymous tips & security reports

  • Anonymous Tips — staff can report concerns confidentially
  • Security Reports — incidents, breaches, theft
  • Both visible only to ADMIN role
  • Investigate within 5 working days

Announcements

  • Push messages to all staff or selected groups
  • Audio playback option for theatre PA
  • Use sparingly — overuse desensitises staff to alerts

Reports for committees

📊 Monthly turnover by theatre
⏱ Cancellation analysis (cause, owner)
💀 Mortality & morbidity (auto)
📦 Inventory burn rate & expiries
⚡ Power reliability summary
🩸 Blood usage patterns

All exportable as PDF for the CMD presentation.

System hygiene checklist (weekly)

  • Approve / reject pending users
  • Review ignored alerts (last 7d)
  • Check expiring stock
  • Review open disciplinary queries (no case > 14 days)
  • Backup database (Supabase auto-snapshots — verify)

# OFFICIAL ANNOUNCEMENT — UNTH THEATRE COMPLEX

**To:** All Heads of Department, Consultants, Resident Doctors, Anaesthetists, Theatre Nurses, Perioperative Staff & Allied Units
**From:** Office of the Director, Theatre Complex
**Date:** 5 June 2026
**Subject:** GO-LIVE — Mandatory Use of the ORM (Operating Room Management) Platform for All Surgical Bookings, Effective **Monday, 8 June 2026**

---

## 1. Effective Date

With effect from **Monday, 8 June 2026**, **ALL** surgical bookings — elective and emergency, across **every surgical unit** — **MUST** be made on the **ORM Platform**. Paper booking lists and informal WhatsApp/phone bookings will **no longer be accepted** by the theatre, anaesthesia team, or pharmacy.

## 2. What Changes on 8 June 2026

### A. Surgical Bookings
- Every case must be booked on the ORM platform by the requesting surgical team.
- The system will automatically:
  - Assign the on-duty anaesthetist from the duty roster.
  - Notify the theatre nurse-in-charge and the pack provider.
  - Generate the surgical pack list, anaesthetic prescription, and pharmacy shopping list.

### B. Surgical Consumable Packs — Patient Payment Flow
1. Patients and their relatives will be **directed to the Surgical Pack Consumable Shop** located:
   > **Upstairs, beside the FCMB ATM, at the office close to the former DA Office.**
2. At the shop, the patient pays for the surgical consumable pack for the scheduled procedure.
3. The cashier issues an **official Evidence of Payment** (receipt).
4. The patient or a member of the surgical team presents this Evidence of Payment **at the theatre** to collect the packed consumables — **already pre-packed and waiting** based on the ORM booking.
5. **No payment is to be collected at the theatre itself.** Theatre staff will only release consumables against a valid Evidence of Payment.

### C. Anaesthetic Prescriptions
- All anaesthetic prescriptions raised by the assigned anaesthetist on ORM will be **wired automatically to the Pharmacy**.
- The pharmacy will prepare and dispense the prescribed drugs **the night before / morning of surgery**, eliminating last-minute prescription rushes.
- The surgical team will collect the dispensed drugs along with the consumable pack in the theatre.

## 3. Why This Change

- **One single source of truth** for every booking — no double-booking, no missing patients on the list.
- **Faster theatre turn-around** — packs and drugs are pre-staged before the patient arrives.
- **Transparent payments** — patients pay at a clearly identified office, with a printed receipt, eliminating informal cash handling in the theatre.
- **Automatic alerts** to the on-duty anaesthetist, nurse, pharmacy, and pack provider — no one is missed.
- **Full audit trail** for every consumable used and every drug dispensed.

## 5. Login & Support

- **Platform:** ORM Theatre Management System
- **Login issues / training:** contact the Theatre IT desk or the System Administrator.
- **Hands-on training sessions:** Friday 5 June & Saturday 6 June — venue to be circulated by your HoD.

## 6. Compliance

From 8 June 2026, any case **not booked on ORM** by **08:00 of the surgery day** will be **deferred** until properly entered. This is to protect patient safety, ensure correct staffing, and guarantee that the required consumables and drugs are ready.
